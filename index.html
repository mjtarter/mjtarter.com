Hello, my name is Matt Tarter and I am a front-end software engineer with 4 years of experience designing, developing, testing, and debugging responsive websites and apps with clean, maintainable code that takes advantage of modern tools and frameworks.

As a self taught programmer, I am a quick learner and an expert with front end technologies including HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Foundation, Responsive development, SEO, Git, and content management systems (specifically Wordpress). I also have experience with a large variety of technologies including PHP, SQL, Ruby on Rails, and Javascript. I have worked for several Denver-based web agencies and my portfolio includes 20+ websites and web apps built on a multitude of platforms for restaurants, doctors, film producers, publicly traded companies, and more.
